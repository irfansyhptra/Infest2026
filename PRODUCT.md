brand

## Users
- **Primary**: IT students, developers, and tech enthusiasts participating in competitions and seminars.
- **Secondary**: Academic staff, sponsors, and industry partners seeking collaboration or talent.
- **Context**: Users visiting to register for events, find schedule details, learn about competitions, and view galleries/results.

## Product Purpose
InFest (Informatics Festival) is an annual tech event celebrating innovation, computing, and technology. The website promotes the event, serves as the main registration portal for competitions and seminars, and displays student work and memories.

## Brand Personality
- **Energetic**: Saturated colors (electric blue and bright gold) evoking high-tech excitement.
- **Professional**: Built upon FMIPA USK's academic foundation, providing clear structure and trust.
- **Innovative**: Bold layout choices and engaging animations showing technology leadership.

## Anti-references
- **Beige/Sand SaaS templates**: Avoid warm-neutral, low-contrast, parchment-like backgrounds (looks like AI-generated boilerplate).
- **Thick card side-borders**: Avoid side-strip borders (e.g., `border-l-2`) on cards.
- **Decorative text gradients**: Avoid using clip-text gradients for body text or sub-headings.
- **Monotonous grid cards**: Avoid identical grids of boxes with simple icon-heading-text styling.

## Design Principles
- **Electric Energy**: Leverage bold primary colors with crisp contrasting text rather than washed-out grays.
- **Functional Motion**: Use GSAP and CSS animations to guide attention and tell stories, not just for decoration.
- **Academic Pride**: Balance USK FMIPA's institutional prestige with high-energy youthful innovation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **Contrast**: Ensure all text meets WCAG AA (≥4.5:1 ratio) against the saturated blue background.
- **Motion Control**: Wrap all high-energy animations in `prefers-reduced-motion` options to avoid disorientation.
- **Multilingual & Accessible**: Cater to local students (Indonesian language primary) with clear, legible text hierarchy.
